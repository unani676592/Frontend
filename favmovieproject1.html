The best movie according to me

My top 3 movies of all the time

"Dune: Part Two"

Directed by Denis Villeneuve, this visually stunning and narratively epic continuation of Frank Herbert's sci-fi series follows Paul Atreides as he allies with Chani and the Fremen while seeking revenge on those who destroyed his family. It has received high praise for its visual effects and storytelling

"Inside Out 2"

This sequel to the beloved Pixar film dives into the emotional life of Riley as she enters her teenage years. The film has been well-received for its emotional intelligence and ability to tackle complex themes with humor and heart

"Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire"

This action-packed entry in the Monsterverse franchise delivers spectacular monster battles. Although it has received mixed reviews, it remains a favorite for its sheer entertainment value
